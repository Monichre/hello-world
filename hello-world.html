A Program to greet the planet.

This page is an attempt to greet everyone on Earth using our various human languages. We're interested to sees how long our list can become.

We'll start with languages at thte Epicodus staff could offer without using Google.

  • english: Hello, World!
  • spanish: Hola, mundo!
  • Japanese: Konnichiwa, sekai!
  • French: Bonjour, monde!
  • Kinyarwanda: Mwirwe, isi!
  • German: Guten tag, Welt!
  • Pig Latin: ello-hay, orld-way!
